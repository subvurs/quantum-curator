Source: Quantum Computing Report May 30, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

These strategic leadership appointments signal a critical maturation phase for the quantum computing industry, as companies transition from research-focused startups to commercially-viable enterprises requiring seasoned operational expertise. Quandela's addition of executives with deep tech scaling experience from established companies like Atos and Eutelsat reflects the growing confidence in photonic quantum computing's commercial potential and the need for proven leadership to navigate international expansion. The recruitment of talent from adjacent high-tech sectors like cybersecurity and aerospace demonstrates how quantum companies are leveraging expertise from industries that have successfully commercialized cutting-edge technologies. This wave of executive appointments across multiple quantum firms suggests the industry is entering a crucial phase where business execution and market strategy are becoming as important as technological breakthroughs.

— Mark Eatherly

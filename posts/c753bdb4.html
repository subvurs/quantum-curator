Quantum Computing Inc. Launches Photonic AI Platform

Source: The Quantum Insider April 23, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article marks a significant step in the convergence of quantum optics and AI computing, as Quantum Computing Inc. transitions their NeuraWave photonic reservoir computing platform from research demonstration to commercial deployment. Photonic computing offers compelling advantages for AI inference tasks, including dramatically reduced energy consumption and the ability to process signals at the speed of light, making it particularly attractive for edge computing applications where power efficiency is critical. While reservoir computing represents a specialized approach to neural network processing rather than full quantum advantage, QCi's move to commercialize this technology demonstrates how quantum optics principles can create practical near-term value in the AI marketplace. The platform's readiness for deployment could signal the beginning of a new category of photonic AI accelerators that bridge classical and quantum computing paradigms.

— Mark Eatherly

Diraq Names Former Broadcom CEO Scott McGregor as Chairman

Source: The Quantum Insider June 09, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This appointment signals Diraq's serious push toward commercialization, leveraging McGregor's deep expertise in CMOS technology and semiconductor manufacturing - precisely the skill set needed to scale their silicon quantum dot approach. McGregor's track record leading major semiconductor companies like Broadcom and his early involvement with foundational computing platforms suggests Diraq is preparing for the transition from research prototype to manufacturable quantum processors. The choice is particularly strategic given Diraq's focus on building quantum computers using modified conventional silicon chips, where traditional semiconductor industry experience becomes invaluable for navigating manufacturing challenges and supply chain scaling.

— Mark Eatherly

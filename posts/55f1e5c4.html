Podcast with Bob Sorensen, Chief Quantum Analyst, Hyperion Research

Source: The Quantum Insider May 16, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This podcast captures a crucial shift happening right now in quantum computing adoption, where the focus is moving from pure research to practical integration with existing high-performance computing infrastructure. Sorensen's problem-first approach represents the maturation of the field, emphasizing that quantum computers should be evaluated as specialized accelerators rather than wholesale replacements for classical systems. The discussion of ROI metrics and procurement strategies signals that HPC centers are seriously preparing for hybrid quantum-classical workflows, which could accelerate real-world quantum applications in optimization, simulation, and machine learning. This practical roadmap for integration is exactly what the industry needs as quantum hardware reaches sufficient maturity to deliver measurable advantages in specific use cases.

— Mark Eatherly

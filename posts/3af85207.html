DARPA Selects Quandela For Stage A of The Quantum Benchmarking Initiative

Source: The Quantum Insider June 16, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

DARPA's selection of Quandela, a French photonic quantum computing company, for its Quantum Benchmarking Initiative represents a significant validation of the photonic approach to quantum computing on the global stage. The initiative's focus on determining whether any architecture can reach utility-scale by 2033 sets a concrete timeline for the field's most pressing question, moving beyond laboratory demonstrations to practical applicability. Quandela's inclusion alongside presumably other leading platforms suggests DARPA is taking a technology-agnostic approach to evaluating which quantum computing methods might actually deliver on real-world problems first. The 2033 target date is particularly noteworthy as it provides the quantum computing community with a clear benchmark for transitioning from experimental systems to machines that can outperform classical computers on commercially relevant tasks.

— Mark Eatherly

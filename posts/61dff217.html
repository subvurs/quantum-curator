India must boost investment in quantum technologies to become world leader, says report

Source: Physics World - Quantum April 28, 2025 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This report from India's National Quantum Mission highlights the increasingly competitive global race for quantum supremacy, with nations recognizing that early investment and strategic planning will determine future technological leadership. India's emphasis on both advancing quantum technologies and strengthening quantum security reflects a mature understanding that quantum computing will simultaneously create new capabilities and vulnerabilities that countries must prepare for simultaneously. The timing is particularly significant as India seeks to position itself alongside the US, China, and EU in quantum research while addressing the critical need for quantum-safe cryptography before large-scale quantum computers threaten current security systems. This dual focus on offensive quantum capabilities and defensive quantum security measures represents exactly the kind of comprehensive national strategy that experts believe will separate quantum leaders from followers in the coming decade.

— Mark Eatherly

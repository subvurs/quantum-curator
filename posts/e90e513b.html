Establishing an Analytical Framework to Benchmark Logical Qubit Performance Claims

Source: Quantum Computing Report June 06, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This Alice & Bob white paper addresses a critical need in quantum computing as companies increasingly announce logical qubit milestones without standardized metrics for comparison. The proposed five-criteria framework could help the field move beyond marketing headlines to meaningful technical assessments, similar to how standardized benchmarks transformed classical computing performance comparisons. As we enter the era of early logical qubits across multiple platforms, having rigorous analytical standards becomes essential for tracking genuine progress toward fault-tolerant quantum computing. This work represents an important step toward establishing the measurement discipline needed to validate whether logical qubit implementations truly surpass the "break-even" threshold where error correction provides net benefit.

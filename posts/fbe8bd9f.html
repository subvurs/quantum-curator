IQM to Deploy Quantum Computer to TOYO in Japan

Source: The Quantum Insider April 27, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This deployment marks a significant milestone as Japan's first enterprise quantum computer installation, positioning IQM's 20-qubit Radiance system at the forefront of the country's quantum computing adoption. The hybrid on-premises and cloud accessibility model reflects the growing trend of making quantum hardware more practically available to businesses, rather than limiting access to research institutions. While 20 qubits represents an intermediate-scale system, this move signals Japan's commitment to building domestic quantum capabilities and could accelerate the development of quantum applications in one of the world's most technology-forward markets. The partnership between Finnish quantum hardware leader IQM and Japanese enterprise TOYO demonstrates how international collaboration is becoming essential for scaling quantum technology deployment globally.

— Mark Eatherly

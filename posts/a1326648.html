ELECTRA AI and Naoris Quantum Protocol Partner on Quantum-Safe Battery Intelligence

Source: The Quantum Insider June 09, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership represents a crucial convergence of quantum-safe cryptography with the rapidly expanding Internet of Things battery ecosystem, addressing a real vulnerability as quantum computers threaten current encryption methods. As AI-powered battery management systems become ubiquitous in everything from electric vehicles to grid storage, securing these always-connected devices against future quantum attacks is becoming a practical necessity rather than a theoretical concern. The collaboration highlights how post-quantum cryptography is moving beyond academic research into commercial applications, particularly in critical infrastructure where compromised battery systems could have cascading effects on power grids and transportation networks. While still early-stage, this type of proactive quantum-safe integration across IoT devices signals the industry's growing recognition that quantum-resistant security needs to be built in from the ground up, not retrofitted later.

— Mark Eatherly

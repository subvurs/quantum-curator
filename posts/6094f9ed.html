Bloq Selected for Funding Under India’s National Quantum Mission to Accelerate Enterprise Quantum Adoption

Source: The Quantum Insider April 13, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights India's serious commitment to building a domestic quantum ecosystem through the National Quantum Mission, with Bloq's selection representing a notable bridge between international quantum expertise and India's growing ambitions in the field. The funding demonstrates how national quantum initiatives are increasingly focusing on practical enterprise applications rather than just fundamental research, signaling a shift toward commercialization across global quantum programs. Bloq's inclusion as the only international startup among nine selected companies suggests India is strategically leveraging external partnerships while nurturing local talent, a model that could accelerate quantum adoption in one of the world's largest technology markets. This development underscores the increasingly competitive global landscape for quantum technology leadership, where nations are actively courting both domestic and international players to build comprehensive quantum capabilities.

— Mark Eatherly

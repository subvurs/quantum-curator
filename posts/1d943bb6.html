Yaqumo, NKT Photonics and Hamamatsu Photonics Sign Quantum Photonics Agreement

Source: The Quantum Insider June 04, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership between Yaqumo, NKT Photonics, and Hamamatsu Photonics addresses a crucial but often overlooked bottleneck in quantum computing development: the supply chain for high-performance optical components. While much attention focuses on qubit count and gate fidelities, neutral atom quantum computers depend heavily on sophisticated laser systems and optical devices for atom trapping, manipulation, and readout. This collaboration signals the quantum industry's maturation beyond pure research, as companies recognize that reliable, industrial-grade optical components are essential for scaling quantum systems from laboratory prototypes to commercial products. The move reflects growing awareness that quantum computing success requires not just better algorithms and processors, but also a robust ecosystem of specialized suppliers and manufacturing partners.

— Mark Eatherly

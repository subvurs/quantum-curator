EPB joins Southeastern Quantum Collaborative to Support Regional Quantum Development

Source: The Quantum Insider April 01, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This announcement signals growing regional coordination in quantum development, with EPB's participation as a telecommunications provider highlighting the crucial role of infrastructure companies in building quantum networks. The Southeastern Quantum Collaborative represents a strategic approach to quantum advancement, pooling resources across universities and industry partners in a region that's been working to establish itself as a quantum hub alongside established centers like Silicon Valley and the Northeast corridor. EPB's involvement is particularly noteworthy given their experience with advanced fiber infrastructure, which will be essential for quantum communication networks and connecting quantum computers across distances. This type of regional collaboration could accelerate practical quantum deployments by creating concentrated expertise and shared resources in specific geographic areas.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Insider Brief PRESS RELEASE — EPB has joined the Southeastern Quantum Collaborative (SQC) as an inaugural member. SQC is an association of universities, technology companies and research institutions working together to accelerate the advancement and real-world application of quantum technologies across the Southeast.

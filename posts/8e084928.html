Alice & Bob Unveils First Quantum System Available For Research Partners

Source: The Quantum Insider June 11, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This announcement marks a significant milestone for Alice & Bob as they transition from pure R&D to delivering complete quantum systems, positioning their unique cat-qubit technology for broader evaluation by research institutions. The Helium system's promise to encode logical qubits with fewer physical qubits than traditional approaches could represent a meaningful advance in quantum error correction efficiency, potentially reducing the massive overhead that has long plagued fault-tolerant quantum computing. While still in the research phase, having an on-premise system available for partners provides crucial real-world testing opportunities that could validate whether cat-qubits can deliver on their theoretical advantages in practical quantum computing applications.

— Mark Eatherly

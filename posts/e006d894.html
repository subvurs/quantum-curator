Canada Gains Access to Pasqal Quantum Systems via PINQ²

Source: The Quantum Insider April 21, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership marks a significant expansion of quantum computing access in Canada, giving researchers and industry partners their first opportunity to work with Pasqal's neutral atom technology alongside IBM's superconducting systems on a single platform. The move is particularly noteworthy because neutral atom quantum computers offer unique advantages for certain optimization problems and can potentially scale more easily than other qubit types, making this a valuable complement to existing quantum infrastructure. Having both technologies available through PINQ² creates an important testing ground for comparing different quantum approaches on real applications, which could accelerate practical quantum advantage discoveries. This also signals growing international competition in quantum technology distribution, as countries race to provide their researchers with access to the most promising quantum hardware platforms.

— Mark Eatherly

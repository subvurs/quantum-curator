Palm Beach State College Takes Aim at Quantum Workforce Demand

Source: The Quantum Insider April 03, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights a critical bottleneck that could determine quantum computing's real-world success: the workforce pipeline. While quantum research has traditionally required PhD-level expertise, the transition to practical quantum applications will demand a much broader range of technicians, engineers, and support personnel with specialized quantum literacy. Palm Beach State College's initiative represents an important shift toward building the mid-level workforce that will be essential for quantum technology deployment and maintenance in industry settings. This type of community college engagement could become a crucial bridge between academic quantum research and the practical skills needed to scale quantum technologies beyond the laboratory.

— Mark Eatherly

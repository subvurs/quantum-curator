QDNL Participations Rebrands to Ground State Ventures as Fund Hits $88 Million, Exceeds Target

Source: The Quantum Insider April 28, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This funding milestone signals growing investor confidence in quantum computing's commercial viability, with Ground State Ventures' oversubscription demonstrating that institutional money is increasingly flowing toward early-stage quantum startups. The $88 million raise, exceeding their $70 million target by over 25%, reflects the maturing quantum investment landscape as venture firms recognize the technology's transition from pure research to potential market applications. This dedicated quantum fund expansion comes at a crucial time when quantum companies need sustained capital to bridge the gap between laboratory breakthroughs and commercially viable products. The rebrand to Ground State Ventures also suggests a more polished, mainstream approach to quantum investing as the field attracts broader financial interest beyond specialized tech investors.

— Mark Eatherly

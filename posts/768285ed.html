IQM Opens First U.S. Quantum Technology Center in Maryland

Source: The Quantum Insider April 10, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

IQM's decision to establish its first U.S. quantum technology center in Maryland's Discovery District represents a significant strategic move that highlights the region's emergence as America's quantum capital. The Finnish quantum computing company, known for its superconducting processors and strong European partnerships, is positioning itself to tap into the rich ecosystem of federal agencies, research institutions, and quantum startups concentrated in the Maryland-Virginia-D.C. corridor. This expansion could accelerate IQM's access to U.S. government contracts and research collaborations while bringing additional European quantum expertise to an already vibrant regional quantum hub. The move underscores how international quantum companies are recognizing the importance of having a physical presence in key quantum ecosystems rather than operating solely from their home countries.

— Mark Eatherly

Report: Quantum Computing Could Unlock More Than £100 Million in Value

Source: The Quantum Insider June 03, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This D-Wave commissioned survey reveals surprisingly bullish short-term expectations among UK enterprises, with nearly half anticipating nine-figure quantum value creation within just one year. While such aggressive timelines may reflect more optimism than current quantum capabilities can deliver, the findings underscore how seriously large organizations are now taking quantum computing as a strategic priority rather than a distant research curiosity. The survey's focus on immediate value creation suggests businesses are primarily looking at quantum annealing applications for optimization problems, where D-Wave's systems have demonstrated real-world advantages today, rather than waiting for fault-tolerant quantum computers that remain years away. This shift from "quantum someday" to "quantum now" thinking, even if overly ambitious, signals a maturing market where quantum solutions are increasingly viewed as practical business tools.

— Mark Eatherly

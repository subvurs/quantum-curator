Australia’s NRFC and Firgun Ventures Inject 40 Million AUD ($28 Million USD) and Growth Capital into Silicon Quantum Computing

Source: Quantum Computing Report June 11, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This funding announcement signals Australia's serious commitment to establishing itself as a major player in the global quantum computing race, with the government-backed National Reconstruction Fund Corporation doubling down on Silicon Quantum Computing's silicon-based qubit approach. What makes this particularly noteworthy is SQC's focus on silicon qubits, which could potentially leverage existing semiconductor manufacturing infrastructure and offer better scalability than some competing technologies. The combination of sovereign funding and international venture capital from Firgun Ventures suggests growing confidence in Australia's quantum ecosystem and SQC's technical progress toward practical quantum processors. This investment underscores how nations are increasingly viewing quantum computing as a strategic technology requiring substantial public-private partnerships to compete on the world stage.

— Mark Eatherly

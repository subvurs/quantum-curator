AI Commentary

This article highlights a strategic Franco‑Qatari partnership that could fast‑track the rollout of photonic quantum computers across the Gulf, leveraging Quandela’s cutting‑edge hardware and Mekdam Holding’s regional influence. By anchoring advanced quantum infrastructure in the GCC, the MoU aligns with recent sovereign initiatives in the Middle East to build homegrown quantum ecosystems for finance, energy and security applications.

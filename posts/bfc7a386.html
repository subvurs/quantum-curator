China’s Quantum Sector Sees Investment Surge as Larger Funding Rounds Return

Source: The Quantum Insider April 06, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

China's quantum sector is experiencing a dramatic funding renaissance, with two startups securing nearly $145 million in venture capital within days of each other - a stark turnaround from the drought that followed when tech giants Alibaba and Baidu retreated from quantum investments. This surge coincides with Beijing's 15th Five-Year Plan officially designating quantum technology as the nation's top "future industry," signaling unprecedented government commitment to quantum leadership on the global stage. The timing is particularly significant as it comes during a period of intense international competition in quantum computing, with China clearly positioning itself to challenge Western dominance in this critical technology sector. These large funding rounds suggest that private investors are betting heavily on China's ability to translate government backing into commercial quantum breakthroughs, potentially accelerating the global quantum race.

— Mark Eatherly

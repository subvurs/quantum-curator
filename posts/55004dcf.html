IQM Quantum Computer Installed at CINECA Supercomputing Center in Italy

Source: The Quantum Insider June 11, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This installation marks a significant step in Europe's quantum computing infrastructure development, positioning CINECA as a key hub for quantum-classical hybrid computing research. The IQM Radiance 54's integration with one of Europe's premier supercomputing centers creates exciting opportunities for researchers to explore quantum advantages in optimization and machine learning applications that require substantial classical computational support. What makes this particularly noteworthy is the strategic placement at CINECA, which serves researchers across multiple European countries and could accelerate collaborative quantum research projects. The timing also coincides with Europe's broader push for quantum sovereignty, reducing dependence on non-European quantum computing platforms while building domestic expertise in this critical technology sector.

— Mark Eatherly

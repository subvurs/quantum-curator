This article matters because it marks one of the first instances where a post‑quantum cryptography suite is being offered through a mainstream cloud marketplace specifically for U.S. intelligence agencies, turning theoretical PQC standards into an immediately deployable service. By placing QuProtect R3 on AWS Marketplace, QuSecure streamlines procurement and integration, helping federal users adopt quantum‑resilient encryption ahead of the NIST standard rollout and reducing supply‑chain risk.

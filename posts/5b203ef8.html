Quantum Systems Accelerator focuses on technologies for computing

Source: Physics World - Quantum February 19, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This Physics World podcast featuring Bert de Jong offers valuable insights into the Quantum Systems Accelerator, a major DOE-funded initiative that's working to bridge the gap between quantum research and practical computing applications. As one of the key figures at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab, de Jong provides an insider's perspective on how national laboratories are coordinating efforts to develop the hardware, software, and algorithmic advances needed to make quantum computing truly useful. The QSA represents a significant policy shift toward more coordinated, application-focused quantum development rather than isolated research efforts. For anyone trying to understand how quantum computing is moving from academic curiosity to engineered reality, this conversation illuminates the strategic thinking behind America's quantum infrastructure investments.

— Mark Eatherly

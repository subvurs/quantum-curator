Quantonation Selects Yaqumo as Its First Japanese Investment

Source: The Quantum Insider April 09, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This investment marks a significant milestone for both the global quantum venture capital landscape and Japan's emerging quantum ecosystem, as Quantonation - the world's largest quantum-focused VC fund - makes its first foray into the Japanese market. Yaqumo's focus on neutral-atom quantum computers positions them in an increasingly competitive but promising architectural approach that companies like QuEra and Pasqal are also pursuing, offering potential advantages in scalability and error correction over traditional superconducting systems. The deal signals growing international recognition of Japan's quantum capabilities beyond just government initiatives, potentially opening doors for more cross-border quantum investments and collaborations. This could be particularly important as Japan works to establish itself as a major player in the global quantum race alongside the US, Europe, and China.

— Mark Eatherly

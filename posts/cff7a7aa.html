OQD Launches Open-Source Collaboration with WD and QuScript to Advance Quantum Error Correction

Source: The Quantum Insider March 26, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This collaboration marks a notable shift toward open-source development in quantum error correction, with Western Digital's involvement particularly significant given their expertise in data storage reliability and error correction in classical systems. The partnership could accelerate practical QEC development by applying WD's proven large-scale error management techniques to quantum systems, where maintaining coherence across thousands of qubits remains one of the field's greatest challenges. Open-source approaches in quantum error correction are still relatively rare compared to hardware development, making this initiative potentially valuable for standardizing QEC protocols and fostering broader industry collaboration. The involvement of a major classical computing company like Western Digital also signals growing recognition that quantum computing's path to commercial viability will require leveraging expertise from traditional tech sectors.

— Mark Eatherly

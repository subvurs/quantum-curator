QUDORA Announces Operational Launch of QUDORA Japan K.K.

Source: The Quantum Insider May 28, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights the continued internationalization of the quantum computing industry as European companies establish stronger footholds in Asia's rapidly growing quantum market. QUDORA's expansion into Japan is particularly strategic given the country's substantial government investments in quantum research and its growing ecosystem of quantum startups and corporate initiatives. The move reflects how quantum computing companies are recognizing that success requires global presence to tap into diverse talent pools, research collaborations, and customer bases across different regions. This geographic expansion trend suggests the quantum industry is maturing beyond its early Silicon Valley and European origins into a truly global technology race.

— Mark Eatherly

Aramco And Pasqal Launch Saudi Arabia’s First Quantum Computer And Middle East’s First Commercial QCaaS Platform

Source: The Quantum Insider May 19, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership marks a significant milestone for quantum computing's global expansion, establishing Saudi Arabia as the first Middle Eastern nation to host a commercial quantum computer and QCaaS platform. Aramco's collaboration with Pasqal, which specializes in neutral-atom quantum systems, positions the oil giant to explore quantum applications in energy optimization, materials science, and complex logistics problems that are central to the petroleum industry. The move reflects Saudi Arabia's broader Vision 2030 initiative to diversify beyond oil, while demonstrating how quantum computing is transitioning from research labs to real-world industrial applications in unexpected geographies. This development could accelerate quantum adoption across the Middle East and establish a new regional hub for quantum research and commercial applications.

— Mark Eatherly

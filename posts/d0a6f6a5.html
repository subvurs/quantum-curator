NPL Collaborates with NVIDIA to Use AI for Automated Quantum Computing Characterization

Source: The Quantum Insider April 21, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This collaboration between NPL and NVIDIA represents a crucial step toward solving one of quantum computing's most persistent challenges: the time-consuming and complex process of characterizing and calibrating quantum devices. By applying AI automation to quantum system characterization, this partnership could dramatically accelerate the development and deployment of quantum computers, which currently require extensive manual tuning and calibration that can take weeks or months. The involvement of the UK's National Metrology Institute signals growing recognition that standardized, automated measurement protocols will be essential for scaling quantum computing from research labs to commercial applications. This work could establish important benchmarks for quantum device performance while making quantum systems more accessible to researchers who lack specialized expertise in quantum characterization.

— Mark Eatherly

Quantum Machines Acquires QHarbor

Source: The Quantum Insider May 05, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This acquisition marks a significant expansion move for Quantum Machines as they establish a footprint in one of Europe's most vibrant quantum hubs, with TU Delft being home to groundbreaking research in quantum computing and networking. The strategic location in Delft positions QM closer to major European quantum initiatives and research institutions, potentially accelerating their hybrid quantum-classical control solutions into new markets. QHarbor's TU Delft origins likely bring valuable academic connections and possibly specialized technology that complements QM's existing quantum control stack. This move reflects the increasingly global nature of the quantum industry, where leading companies are positioning themselves near key research centers to tap into talent and collaboration opportunities.

— Mark Eatherly

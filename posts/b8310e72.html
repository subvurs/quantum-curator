Infleqtion and Safran Launch Integrated Quantum Timing Solution for GNSS-Challenged Environments

Source: Quantum Computing Report April 04, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership between Infleqtion and Safran represents a significant step toward commercializing quantum sensing technology for real-world applications where GPS signals are unreliable or unavailable. By integrating quantum optical clocks with proven synchronization platforms, the collaboration addresses a critical vulnerability in modern infrastructure that relies heavily on satellite-based timing systems. The focus on defense and telecommunications sectors highlights how quantum technologies are moving beyond research labs to solve immediate operational challenges, particularly in environments where adversaries might jam or spoof GPS signals. This type of quantum-classical hybrid approach could become a template for making other quantum sensing technologies commercially viable in the near term.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Infleqtion (NYSE: INFQ), a specialist in neutral-atom quantum technology, and Safran Electronics & Defense have announced the commercial availability of a quantum-enabled precision timing solution. The system integrates Infleqtion’s Tiqker™ quantum optical clock with Safran’s established White Rabbit and SecureSync® synchronization platforms.

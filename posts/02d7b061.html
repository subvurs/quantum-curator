15+ Global Banks Exploring Quantum Technologies [2026]

Source: The Quantum Insider March 27, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights the growing recognition among major financial institutions that quantum computing represents both an opportunity and an existential threat to current banking infrastructure. While quantum technologies promise revolutionary improvements in fraud detection, risk modeling, and transaction processing through their ability to handle massive datasets, banks are also racing to implement quantum-resistant cryptography before quantum computers can break current encryption methods. The fact that over 15 global banks are actively exploring these technologies signals that quantum computing has moved from theoretical curiosity to strategic imperative in the financial sector. This dual-edged quantum revolution in banking could reshape everything from high-frequency trading algorithms to the fundamental security protocols that protect trillions of dollars in daily transactions.

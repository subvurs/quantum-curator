BTQ Technologies Completes Acquisition of QPerfect to Expand Quantum Software Capabilities

Source: The Quantum Insider July 08, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

AI Commentary

This acquisition gives BTQ a full‑stack software layer—emulation, digital‑twin modeling, and low‑level control—that many hardware‑focused quantum firms still lack, positioning it to offer end‑to‑end solutions for secure communications networks. By folding QPerfect’s expertise into its portfolio, BTQ can accelerate the testing and validation of quantum protocols without needing costly physical qubits, a trend echoed by recent moves from IBM and Microsoft to strengthen their software ecosystems. The deal could shorten time‑to‑market for quantum‑enhanced security products, though successful integration will be key as the market remains crowded with larger players vying for similar capabilities.

— Mark Eatherly

Firgun Ventures Announces First Australian Investment in Silicon Quantum Computing

Source: The Quantum Insider June 11, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This investment marks a significant milestone for Australia's quantum computing ecosystem, as Firgun Ventures recognizes Silicon Quantum Computing's potential in the highly competitive race to build scalable quantum processors. SQC's silicon-based approach is particularly noteworthy because it leverages existing semiconductor manufacturing infrastructure, potentially offering a more practical path to large-scale quantum computers compared to other qubit technologies that require exotic materials or extreme operating conditions. The backing from both international venture capital and Australia's National Reconstruction Fund Corporation signals growing confidence in silicon quantum computing as a viable commercial pathway, especially important as the field moves beyond laboratory demonstrations toward practical applications. This cross-border investment also reflects the increasingly global nature of quantum technology development, where leading approaches are attracting capital regardless of geographic boundaries.

— Mark Eatherly

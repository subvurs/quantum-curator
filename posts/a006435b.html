Photonic Inc. Closes Investment Round With Over $200 Million USD

Source: The Quantum Insider May 12, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This massive $200 million funding round for Photonic Inc. signals serious institutional confidence in photonic quantum computing as a viable path forward, especially noteworthy given the current challenging funding environment for quantum startups. The company's focus on distributed quantum computing using photonic systems represents a compelling alternative to trapped ion and superconducting approaches, potentially offering advantages in networking quantum processors and operating at room temperature. With a $2 billion post-money valuation, Photonic now ranks among the most highly valued pure-play quantum computing companies, reflecting investor belief that photonic approaches could unlock practical quantum networks and distributed computing architectures that other platforms struggle to achieve.

— Mark Eatherly

Insider Brief PRESS RELEASE — Photonic Inc., a global leader in distributed quantum computing, today announced the final close of over $200M USD ($275M CAD) in investment, giving the company a $2B USD ($2.7B CAD) post-money valuation. The round, led by Planet First Partners, a UK-based sustainable technology growth equity firm, brings total capital raised by Photonic to over $350M USD ($475M CAD).

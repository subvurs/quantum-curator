KIST Accelerates U.S. Expansion of Quantum Deep-Tech Startups Through SelectUSA 2026

Source: The Quantum Insider May 06, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This initiative represents a significant strategic move by South Korea to position itself as a major player in the global quantum ecosystem, leveraging government-backed support to help domestic quantum startups establish footholds in the crucial U.S. market. The collaboration between KIST and Korea's Ministry of SMEs highlights how nations are increasingly viewing quantum technology as a critical economic and security asset requiring coordinated public-private partnerships. While the U.S. has dominated quantum investment and talent acquisition, South Korea's systematic approach through programs like the Deeptech Project signals intensifying international competition for quantum leadership. This cross-border expansion effort could accelerate global quantum innovation by bringing diverse technological approaches and fresh perspectives to the established U.S. quantum hub ecosystem.

— Mark Eatherly

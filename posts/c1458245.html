Quantum Threat to Crypto Is Not Here Yet, but Coinbase Advisory Board Says the Time to Prepare Is Now

Source: The Quantum Insider June 13, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This Coinbase-commissioned report marks a significant shift in the cryptocurrency industry's approach to quantum threats, moving from speculative timelines to practical preparation strategies. While cryptographically relevant quantum computers remain years away, the report wisely emphasizes that transitioning billions of dollars in cryptocurrency infrastructure to quantum-resistant algorithms will require extensive planning and gradual implementation. The timing is particularly crucial since blockchain systems, once deployed, are notoriously difficult to upgrade due to their decentralized nature and the need for consensus among participants. This proactive stance from a major crypto exchange signals that the industry is finally taking seriously the long-term challenge of maintaining security in a post-quantum world.

— Mark Eatherly

Cisco Advances Quantum Networking with New Universal Quantum Switch

Source: The Quantum Insider April 23, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This announcement marks a significant step toward practical quantum networking infrastructure, as Cisco tackles one of the field's most pressing challenges: how to route and switch quantum information without destroying its delicate quantum properties. The Universal Quantum Switch represents a crucial piece of the puzzle for building large-scale quantum networks, potentially enabling secure quantum communication and distributed quantum computing across vast distances. While still in the research prototype phase, Cisco's entry into quantum networking hardware demonstrates how traditional networking giants are positioning themselves for the quantum internet era, bringing their decades of classical networking expertise to bear on quantum's unique requirements. This development could accelerate the timeline for quantum networking applications, from ultra-secure communications to connecting remote quantum computers into powerful distributed systems.

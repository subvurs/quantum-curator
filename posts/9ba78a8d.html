Podcast with Tom Darras, CEO and Co-founder, Welinq

Source: Quantum Computing Report March 31, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This podcast offers valuable insights into quantum networking, a critical but often overlooked piece of the quantum computing puzzle that could solve the scaling challenges facing current quantum processors. Welinq's focus on quantum memories and entanglement distribution represents a key enabling technology for creating distributed quantum computing clusters, potentially allowing smaller quantum processors to work together as larger, more powerful systems. The discussion of quantum-safe connectivity between data centers is particularly timely as organizations begin preparing for the post-quantum cryptography transition. Tom Darras provides a clear technical breakdown of the building blocks needed to make quantum networks practical, making this essential listening for understanding how quantum computing might scale beyond individual machines.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Yuval Boger interviews Tom Darras, CEO and co-founder of Welinq. They discuss how quantum networking uses shared entanglement to interconnect quantum processors, enabling modular scale-out clusters and quantum-safe connectivity between data centers.

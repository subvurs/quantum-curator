What Quantum Technology Actually is and Why it Matters

Source: The Quantum Insider March 24, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article addresses a critical communication challenge facing the quantum industry: the tendency to oversimplify "quantum technology" as synonymous with quantum computing alone. While quantum computers capture headlines, quantum sensing and quantum communications are already delivering real-world applications in everything from medical imaging to secure financial transactions, yet they often get overlooked in mainstream discussions. As governments worldwide develop quantum strategies and allocate billions in funding, having precise terminology becomes essential for effective policy-making and public understanding. The piece serves as a valuable primer for distinguishing between quantum computing's future promises and the quantum technologies already transforming industries today.

— Mark Eatherly

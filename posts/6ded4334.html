Toshiba and LQUOM Partner on Long-Distance Quantum Communication Research

Source: The Quantum Insider March 30, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership between Toshiba and LQUOM addresses one of the most pressing challenges in quantum communication: maintaining quantum key distribution security over long distances where quantum signals naturally degrade. Toshiba brings deep expertise in QKD systems and has been a pioneer in quantum cryptography for over two decades, while LQUOM specializes in quantum repeater technologies essential for extending quantum networks. The collaboration is particularly significant as it could help bridge the gap between current metropolitan-scale quantum networks and the ultimate goal of intercontinental quantum communication infrastructure. Success in long-distance QKD extension would be a crucial step toward building the quantum internet that governments and enterprises need for truly unbreakable communications.

— Mark Eatherly

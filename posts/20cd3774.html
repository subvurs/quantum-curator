Fixstars Amplify Adds QUDORA’s Quantum Computers to its Standard Machines

Source: The Quantum Insider June 01, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership represents a significant step in making quantum computing more accessible to Japanese enterprises, as Fixstars Amplify's optimization platform now integrates QUDORA's quantum cloud services for the first time in Japan. The collaboration is particularly noteworthy because it bridges the gap between quantum hardware and practical optimization applications, allowing businesses to leverage quantum algorithms through Amplify's user-friendly interface without needing deep quantum expertise. This type of platform integration is becoming increasingly important as the quantum industry matures, enabling companies to experiment with quantum optimization solutions for real-world problems like logistics, scheduling, and resource allocation. The move also highlights the growing trend of quantum startups partnering with established software platforms to accelerate commercial adoption of quantum technologies.

— Mark Eatherly

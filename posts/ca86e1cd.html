Haiqu Launches Agentic Quantum Operating System For Enterprise Applications R&D

Source: The Quantum Insider May 06, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This announcement represents a significant step toward making quantum computing more accessible to enterprises by addressing one of the field's biggest bottlenecks: the complexity of quantum software development. Haiqu's "agentic" approach suggests they're incorporating AI-driven automation to help bridge the gap between quantum hardware capabilities and practical business applications, potentially reducing the steep learning curve that has kept many companies on the sidelines. The focus on a full-stack solution is particularly noteworthy, as most quantum software efforts have been fragmented across different layers of the computing stack. If successful, this type of integrated platform could accelerate the timeline for quantum advantage in real-world enterprise scenarios by making quantum development more like conventional software engineering.

— Mark Eatherly

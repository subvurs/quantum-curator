IQM and Real Asset Acquisition Corp. Announce Upsized $146 Million PIPE with New Commitment from Ilmarinen

Source: The Quantum Insider June 02, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article signals growing institutional confidence in quantum hardware companies as pension fund Ilmarinen joins IQM's expanded $146 million PIPE financing alongside their SPAC merger. The upsized funding round demonstrates that major institutional investors are increasingly willing to back quantum computing companies through the challenging path from R&D to commercialization, particularly those like IQM that focus on full-stack superconducting quantum systems. While SPAC deals in quantum have had mixed results, the additional commitment from a major Finnish pension fund suggests sophisticated investors see real value in IQM's European quantum computing approach and customer traction. This funding boost should help IQM accelerate their hardware development and compete more effectively with established players like IBM and emerging rivals in the increasingly crowded quantum hardware space.

— Mark Eatherly

On the path towards a quantum economy

Source: Physics World - Quantum April 16, 2025 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights a crucial phase in quantum computing's evolution where the field is transitioning from pure research to practical business applications through systematic feasibility studies. These collaborative efforts between quantum specialists and industry experts represent a vital bridge-building exercise, helping companies understand which quantum computing advantages might genuinely benefit their operations versus areas where classical computing remains superior. The emphasis on feasibility studies is particularly significant because it suggests the quantum computing industry is maturing beyond the hype cycle into rigorous, evidence-based assessments of where quantum advantage can realistically emerge. This collaborative approach could accelerate the timeline for meaningful quantum applications by ensuring development efforts focus on problems where quantum computers can deliver measurable business value rather than pursuing quantum solutions simply for their novelty.

— Mark Eatherly

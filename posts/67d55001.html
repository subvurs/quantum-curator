Pasqal Showcases Quantum Progress at 2026 Event

Source: The Quantum Insider April 21, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This event highlights how quantum computing is increasingly becoming a global diplomatic and economic priority, with Pasqal strategically positioning itself at the center of international quantum conversations. The timing on World Quantum Day and diverse international attendance spanning government, finance, and industry demonstrates that neutral-atom quantum computing platforms like Pasqal's are gaining serious traction beyond just technical circles. While the article appears to be largely ceremonial, the fact that a quantum startup can draw 150+ high-level participants from 10+ countries suggests the field is maturing from pure research into a technology with geopolitical implications. The Paris setting also reinforces Europe's growing ambitions to compete with US and Chinese quantum initiatives through companies like Pasqal.

— Mark Eatherly

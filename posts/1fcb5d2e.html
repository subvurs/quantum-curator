Curator's Take

This article highlights a critical strategic concern as Europe prepares its Quantum Act legislation while watching the continent fall behind in the AI race. The piece underscores the urgent need for European policymakers to balance necessary quantum regulations with the speed and agility required to compete globally with the US and China, both of which are making massive quantum investments. This represents a pivotal moment where Europe's quantum ambitions will either translate into real competitive advantage or become another cautionary tale of bureaucratic inertia stifling technological progress.

Florida’s First Quantum-Safe Corridor – LambdaRail and the New Shape of the State’s Quantum Push

Source: The Quantum Insider April 28, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership between IonQ and Florida LambdaRail represents a significant shift from quantum computing hype to practical infrastructure deployment, as Florida builds its first quantum-safe communications corridor. The timing is particularly noteworthy as organizations worldwide are scrambling to prepare for the post-quantum cryptography transition before quantum computers mature enough to break current encryption methods. By establishing this quantum-safe network infrastructure now, Florida is positioning itself as a leader in both quantum technology adoption and cybersecurity preparedness, potentially attracting quantum companies and research institutions to the state. This type of proactive infrastructure development could serve as a model for other states looking to build quantum-ready ecosystems rather than simply chasing quantum startups.

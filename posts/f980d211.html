SEALSQ Deepens Partnership with Quantum Computing Startup EeroQ

Source: The Quantum Insider June 01, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership highlights the growing convergence between quantum computing hardware development and the urgent need for quantum-resistant cybersecurity solutions. SEALSQ's investment in EeroQ's electron-on-helium quantum architecture is particularly noteworthy because this CMOS-compatible approach could potentially offer a more practical path to scalable quantum processors compared to traditional superconducting or trapped-ion systems. The collaboration makes strategic sense as SEALSQ's expertise in post-quantum cryptography pairs naturally with EeroQ's novel quantum computing platform, potentially creating an integrated solution that addresses both sides of the quantum revolution. While electron-on-helium qubits remain relatively early-stage compared to other quantum technologies, this partnership signals serious industry investment in alternative quantum computing approaches that could prove more manufacturable using existing semiconductor infrastructure.

— Mark Eatherly

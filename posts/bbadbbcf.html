The Korea Quantum Trade: Why Seoul Produced the Biggest Stock Moves on NVIDIA’s Ising Launch

Source: The Quantum Insider April 18, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights a fascinating intersection between AI and quantum computing, where NVIDIA's new Ising models are designed to solve optimization problems that quantum computers are expected to excel at in the future. The dramatic stock movements on Korea's KOSDAQ rather than US markets suggests that Korean quantum companies and investors may be more attuned to the long-term implications of classical AI systems tackling quantum-style problems. This development is particularly significant because it demonstrates how the boundaries between classical and quantum computing continue to blur, with companies like NVIDIA positioning themselves at the crossroads of both technologies. The market reaction also reveals growing global awareness that quantum advantage may emerge gradually through hybrid classical-quantum approaches rather than through pure quantum systems alone.

— Mark Eatherly

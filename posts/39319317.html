Diraq Expands U.S. Presence with Palo Alto Office

Source: The Quantum Insider June 19, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article matters because Diraq’s new Palo Alto office underscores the growing momentum behind silicon‑based quantum processors, a platform that promises tighter integration with existing semiconductor supply chains. By planting itself in the heart of Silicon Valley, Diraq can more readily forge collaborations with chip manufacturers and venture investors, accelerating its roadmap toward utility‑scale machines that could compete with superconducting approaches from IBM and Google. The move is a clear signal that industry players are betting on hardware scalability now, even though practical quantum advantage remains several development cycles away.

— Mark Eatherly

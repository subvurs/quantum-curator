Moth Expands Into The US With Senior Hires From Amazon And IBM to Accelerate Growth

Source: The Quantum Insider April 07, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This expansion represents a notable shift in the quantum computing landscape as UK-based Moth seeks to establish a stronger foothold in the competitive US market by recruiting talent from tech giants Amazon and IBM. The hiring of senior executives with experience at these quantum computing powerhouses suggests Moth is positioning itself to bridge the gap between cutting-edge quantum research and practical business applications. While personnel moves alone don't guarantee success, the company's focus on making quantum computing "accessible to businesses and consumers" indicates another player is entering the race to commercialize quantum technologies for mainstream adoption. This move reflects the increasingly global nature of quantum competition, with companies seeking top talent across borders to accelerate their quantum ambitions.

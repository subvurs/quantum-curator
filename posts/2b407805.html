Infleqtion Selected for DARPA HARQ Quantum Computing Project

Source: The Quantum Insider April 22, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This DARPA HARQ contract represents a significant validation of Infleqtion's neutral-atom quantum computing approach, which has been gaining momentum as a compelling alternative to superconducting and trapped-ion systems. The HARQ program specifically focuses on heterogeneous quantum architectures that can combine different qubit technologies, suggesting DARPA sees real potential in hybrid systems that leverage the unique strengths of various quantum platforms. Infleqtion's Multistaq platform development under this contract could accelerate the practical deployment of quantum systems by creating more flexible, modular architectures that aren't locked into a single technological approach. The $2 million investment, while modest by defense standards, signals growing government confidence in neutral-atom quantum technology for national security applications.

— Mark Eatherly

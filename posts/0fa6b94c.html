NRFC Investment Accelerates SQC’s Atomic-Scale Manufacturing

Source: The Quantum Insider March 24, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This investment marks a significant milestone for Silicon Quantum Computing's unique atomic-scale manufacturing approach, which positions them as the sole company worldwide capable of fabricating quantum chips with atomic precision. The AUD$20 million from Australia's National Reconstruction Fund Corporation not only validates SQC's silicon-based quantum technology but also strengthens Australia's position in the global quantum race, particularly in the critical hardware manufacturing sector where precision at the atomic level remains one of the field's greatest technical challenges. What makes this particularly noteworthy is SQC's claim of already delivering quantum-enhanced AI products to enterprise and government clients today, suggesting their technology has moved beyond pure research into practical applications. This combination of manufacturing precision and commercial readiness could accelerate the timeline for practical quantum computing deployment, especially in applications where silicon-based quantum systems offer advantages over other qubit technologies.

— Mark Eatherly

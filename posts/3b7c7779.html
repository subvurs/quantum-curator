Quantum Systems Accelerator focuses on technologies for computing

Source: Physics World - Quantum February 19, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This Physics World podcast episode with Bert de Jong provides valuable insights into the Quantum Systems Accelerator (QSA), a major US Department of Energy initiative that's working to bridge the gap between current quantum research and practical quantum computing applications. As co-director of this national quantum center, de Jong offers a unique perspective on how the QSA is tackling critical challenges like quantum error correction, algorithm development, and hardware integration across multiple technology platforms. The discussion likely covers the accelerator's collaborative approach, bringing together national labs, universities, and industry partners to advance quantum technologies that could eventually transform scientific computing and materials discovery. For anyone following quantum policy and large-scale research initiatives, this conversation illuminates how government-funded programs are strategically positioning the US in the global quantum race.

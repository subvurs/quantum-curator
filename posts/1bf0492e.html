EPB Quantum to Host First Quantum in Business Event in Chattanooga

Source: The Quantum Insider June 16, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

EPB Quantum's decision to host a business-focused quantum conference in Chattanooga signals the technology's shift from pure research to practical commercial adoption, particularly in traditionally conservative industries like finance and insurance. The $2.7 trillion economic value projection underscores growing confidence that near-term quantum applications can deliver real business value, even before fault-tolerant systems arrive. What makes this especially noteworthy is the venue choice: holding the event in a mid-sized southern city rather than traditional tech hubs like San Francisco or Boston suggests quantum computing's economic impact is expected to be geographically distributed. The focus on first-mover advantage reflects an industry maturation moment where companies need to start building quantum literacy and partnerships now rather than waiting for the technology to fully mature.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Insider Brief PRESS RELEASE — On October 1, 2026, EPB Quantum will host the inaugural Quantum in Business (QiB) conference, exclusively in Chattanooga, Tennessee. QiB will highlight how companies can benefit from a projected $2.7 trillion in economic value by utilizing quantum computing in the near-term, especially in financial, healthcare, insurance, chemicals, logistics and automotive industries.

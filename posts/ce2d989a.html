Quandela Adds Industry Veterans to Leadership And Governance Teams

Source: The Quantum Insider May 29, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article signals Quandela's transition from a promising quantum startup to a company positioning for commercial scale-up, with leadership appointments that bring deep operational and industrialization expertise from major tech companies like OVHcloud. The addition of veterans focused on manufacturing and operations suggests Quandela is moving beyond research milestones toward building production-ready photonic quantum systems, which is particularly significant given photonics' potential advantages in room-temperature operation and networking applications. These strategic hires reflect the broader maturation of the quantum computing industry, where companies are now prioritizing commercial execution alongside technological innovation. The emphasis on industrialization expertise specifically indicates Quandela may be preparing for larger-scale deployment of their photonic quantum computers in the coming years.

— Mark Eatherly

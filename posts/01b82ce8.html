Capital of Quantum Welcomes Quantum Motion to Discovery District Maryland

Source: The Quantum Insider June 15, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

Quantum Motion's expansion to Maryland's Discovery District signals the growing gravitational pull of regional quantum hubs beyond traditional tech centers. The UK-based company's silicon CMOS approach is particularly noteworthy because it leverages existing semiconductor manufacturing infrastructure, potentially offering a more scalable path to large-scale quantum computers than exotic materials requiring extreme conditions. Maryland has been strategically positioning itself as America's quantum capital through coordinated investment in quantum startups, federal research partnerships with nearby agencies like NIST, and university programs, creating a geographic clustering effect similar to what happened with biotech in Boston. This move reflects a broader trend of quantum hardware companies establishing international footprints to tap into diverse talent pools and funding ecosystems while staying close to government customers and research collaborators.

— Mark Eatherly

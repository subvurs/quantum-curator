Terra Quantum and Merqury Deploy Live Post-Quantum Secure Optical Network Link in Malta

Source: Quantum Computing Report May 26, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This deployment represents a significant step toward real-world quantum-secure communications, moving QKD technology from laboratory demonstrations to actual commercial telecommunications infrastructure. The collaboration between Terra Quantum, Merqury Cybersecurity, and network operator Melita Business demonstrates how quantum security solutions are beginning to integrate with existing network operators and data centers, creating a practical pathway for broader adoption. What makes this particularly noteworthy is that it's running on a live production network rather than a test environment, suggesting the technology has reached sufficient maturity for commercial deployment. As organizations prepare for the eventual threat posed by quantum computers to current encryption methods, projects like this Malta installation provide crucial real-world validation of quantum-safe networking solutions.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Terra Quantum AG has successfully implemented its proprietary Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) hardware platform onto a live, production-grade telecommunications network in Malta. Executed in technical collaboration with local developer Merqury Cybersecurity and network operator Melita Business, the deployment establishes a post-quantum secure data link connecting two primary commercial data centers.

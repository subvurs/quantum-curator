Rigetti Computing Intends to Invest $100 Million in UK to Accelerate Quantum Computing Development

Source: The Quantum Insider March 27, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This investment marks a significant milestone for Rigetti as it becomes their first major expansion beyond the US market, signaling the company's confidence in the UK's growing quantum ecosystem and talent pool. The $100 million commitment positions Rigetti to tap into Britain's strong quantum research infrastructure, including partnerships with universities and access to government-backed quantum initiatives like the National Quantum Computing Centre. This move reflects the increasingly global nature of quantum computing development, where companies are seeking the best talent and research environments regardless of geography. The investment could accelerate quantum commercialization efforts by leveraging the UK's robust quantum research community while potentially creating new pathways for European quantum computing applications.

— Mark Eatherly

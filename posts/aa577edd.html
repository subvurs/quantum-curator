Multipeak and RAQS Partner on Quantum Deployment

Source: The Quantum Insider April 08, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership represents a crucial step in quantum computing's evolution from laboratory curiosities to real-world applications, particularly in the often-overlooked challenge of commercial deployment. By combining Multipeak's commercialization expertise with RAQS Quantum's scientific capabilities, the collaboration addresses the notorious "valley of death" where promising quantum technologies struggle to make the leap to practical business solutions. The focus on Asian and European markets is especially significant, as these regions are investing heavily in quantum infrastructure and seeking "quantum-ready" solutions that can provide immediate value while preparing for future quantum advantages. This type of industry-academic bridge is exactly what the quantum sector needs to move beyond research prototypes toward scalable commercial products.

— Mark Eatherly

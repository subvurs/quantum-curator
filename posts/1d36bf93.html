Qubits Ventures Hosts Quantum Event in Pasadena

Source: The Quantum Insider April 02, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This networking event highlights the growing maturation of quantum computing as an investment sector, with dedicated venture capital firms like Qubits Ventures now actively cultivating the startup ecosystem. The choice of Caltech as the venue is particularly strategic, given the institution's leading role in quantum research and its proximity to both Silicon Valley tech culture and emerging quantum companies. While networking events themselves don't advance the technology, they serve as crucial catalysts for connecting researchers, entrepreneurs, and investors who can accelerate the translation of quantum breakthroughs from lab to market. The timing around World Quantum Day also reflects the field's increasing efforts to build public awareness and attract talent beyond traditional academic circles.

— Mark Eatherly

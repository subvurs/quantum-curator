This article matters because Arq’s $1.4 million pre‑seed raise signals growing investor confidence that a dedicated quantum‑internet stack—hardware, software and networking protocols—is needed to move beyond isolated quantum processors. While the venture highlights real momentum, readers should remember that building a global quantum internet still faces major engineering and standards challenges before widespread adoption.

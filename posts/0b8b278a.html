ORCA Computing Joins Digital Realty Innovation Lab in London

Source: The Quantum Insider June 12, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership represents a crucial step toward making quantum computing more commercially viable by addressing one of the field's biggest practical challenges: integrating quantum systems into existing data center infrastructure. ORCA Computing's photonic quantum computers are particularly well-suited for this type of testing environment since they can operate at room temperature, unlike many superconducting quantum systems that require elaborate cooling setups. The collaboration with Digital Realty, a major data center operator, signals that the industry is moving beyond laboratory demonstrations toward real-world deployment scenarios where quantum and classical systems must work seamlessly together. This kind of infrastructure partnership could accelerate the timeline for quantum computing applications by providing a controlled environment to work out integration challenges before full commercial rollouts.

— Mark Eatherly

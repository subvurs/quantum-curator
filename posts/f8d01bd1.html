Monarch Quantum Surpasses $100 Million in Capital and Contracts with $55 Million Growth Round to Scale Quantum Photonics Infrastructure

Source: The Quantum Insider March 31, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This significant funding milestone demonstrates the growing investor confidence in photonic quantum computing approaches, with Monarch Quantum's rapid ascent from founding in 2025 to surpassing $100 million in combined capital and contracts highlighting the accelerating commercialization timeline in quantum tech. The involvement of 55 North, the world's largest dedicated quantum fund, alongside other deep tech investors signals strong institutional backing for photonics-based quantum infrastructure, which offers potential advantages in terms of room-temperature operation and networking capabilities compared to superconducting approaches. The "oversubscribed" nature of this growth round reflects the intense competition among investors to back promising quantum hardware companies as the field moves toward practical applications. This funding will likely accelerate Monarch's development of integrated photonics platforms that could play a crucial role in scaling quantum computing and quantum communication networks.

— Mark Eatherly

Northwest Quantum Nexus Hosts Quantum Day Event in Seattle

Source: The Quantum Insider April 11, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This regional quantum summit represents a crucial step in building the distributed quantum ecosystem that will be essential for the technology's commercial success. While major quantum hubs like Boston and Silicon Valley often dominate headlines, the Northwest's growing quantum presence - anchored by companies like Microsoft, Amazon, and emerging startups - demonstrates how quantum innovation is spreading beyond traditional tech centers. The Northwest Quantum Nexus initiative highlights an important trend where regional coalitions are forming to coordinate quantum workforce development, supply chain partnerships, and policy advocacy across state lines. Such regional collaboration could prove vital for creating the infrastructure and talent pipelines needed to support quantum technologies as they transition from laboratory curiosities to practical applications.

— Mark Eatherly

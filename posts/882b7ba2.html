Pasqal and MegazoneCloud Partner to Bring Quantum Computing to South Korea

Source: The Quantum Insider July 07, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

AI Commentary

This article marks the first time a neutral‑atom quantum processor is being paired with a major Korean cloud provider, signaling that the ecosystem of “quantum‑as‑a‑service” is maturing beyond the usual superconducting offerings from AWS, Azure and Google. By leveraging MegazoneCloud’s AI‑focused infrastructure, Pasqal hopes to fast‑track enterprise pilots in South Korea, a market that has recently rolled out generous R&D incentives for quantum technologies. The partnership could broaden commercial deployment pathways for neutral‑atom hardware, but its impact will hinge on how quickly the platform can scale qubit counts and integrate with existing cloud software stacks.

— Mark Eatherly

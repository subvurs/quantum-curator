UK Launches SPOQC Satellite for Quantum Communications Research

Source: The Quantum Insider March 31, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This satellite launch represents a crucial step toward building truly global quantum communication networks, as space-based quantum key distribution could eventually enable unhackable communications between any two points on Earth. The SPOQC mission builds on years of ground-based quantum communication research and moves the technology into the challenging space environment, where quantum signals must survive the harsh conditions of orbital deployment. While current quantum communication is limited by the distance light can travel through fiber optic cables before quantum states degrade, satellites could potentially relay quantum-encrypted messages across continents without the security vulnerabilities of traditional methods. The UK's investment in this space-based quantum infrastructure positions the nation as a leader in the emerging quantum internet, though practical applications will likely require several more years of development and testing.

— Mark Eatherly

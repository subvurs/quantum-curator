memQ Raises $10 Million in Series A

Source: The Quantum Insider March 31, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This funding round highlights the growing recognition that quantum networking infrastructure is just as crucial as quantum processors themselves for realizing distributed quantum computing's full potential. memQ's focus on quantum networking solutions addresses a critical bottleneck in scaling quantum systems beyond single machines, enabling quantum computers to work together across distances and potentially unlocking applications that require more qubits than any single device can provide. The strong investor interest from quantum-focused funds like Quantonation signals that the industry is maturing beyond just hardware development to encompass the entire quantum computing stack. This investment positions memQ to help build the "quantum internet" backbone that will be essential for connecting quantum devices and creating truly distributed quantum computing networks.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Insider Brief PRESS RELEASE — memQ™, the industry leader in quantum networking solutions for distributed quantum computing, announced today the closing of its $10M Series A round of financing. The round was co-led by Quantonation and Ocean Azul Partners, with syndicate participation from both existing and new investors.

Podcast with John Martinis, 2025 Nobel Prize Winner and CTO of Qolab

Source: Quantum Computing Report March 28, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article provides rare insights from John Martinis, whose pioneering work on superconducting qubits laid the foundation for Google's quantum supremacy achievement and much of today's quantum hardware landscape. His transition from academic research to leading Qolab as CTO represents a significant trend of quantum luminaries moving into the startup ecosystem, bringing deep technical expertise to commercialize next-generation quantum technologies. Given Martinis' track record in developing some of the world's most advanced quantum processors, his perspective on the current state of the field and Qolab's technical approach offers valuable intelligence on where quantum hardware development is headed. The timing is particularly noteworthy as the quantum industry faces critical scaling challenges that require exactly the kind of foundational expertise Martinis brings to bear.

— Mark Eatherly

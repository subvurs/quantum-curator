QCi Deploys Dirac-3 System on Quantum Corridor Network

Source: The Quantum Insider March 30, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This deployment marks a significant step toward practical quantum computing infrastructure, as QCi's Dirac-3 optimization system becomes accessible through North America's first quantum-safe commercial network. The partnership between Quantum Corridor and QCi demonstrates how quantum hardware companies are moving beyond lab demonstrations to create real commercial access points for enterprises needing quantum optimization capabilities. While QCi's photonic approach to quantum optimization differs from gate-based quantum computers, this network deployment could provide valuable real-world testing for quantum algorithms in logistics, finance, and supply chain optimization. The integration with quantum-safe communications also highlights the growing ecosystem where quantum computing and quantum cryptography technologies are being bundled together for enterprise customers.

— Mark Eatherly

Chattanooga Launches Quantum Workforce Pre-Apprenticeship Program

Source: The Quantum Insider May 07, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This marks a significant milestone in quantum computing's transition from academic curiosity to industrial reality, as Chattanooga becomes the first U.S. city to launch a formal quantum workforce development program. The 12-week paid pre-apprenticeship represents a crucial bridge between the growing demand for quantum-skilled professionals and the current talent shortage that threatens to bottleneck the industry's commercialization efforts. While quantum hardware continues advancing rapidly, the human infrastructure needed to deploy these technologies has lagged behind, making workforce development initiatives like this essential for maintaining America's competitive edge in the quantum race. This practical approach to quantum education could serve as a model for other regions looking to position themselves as quantum innovation hubs.

— Mark Eatherly

SpinQ Technology Secures Nearly 1 Billion Chinese Yuan in Series C Funding to Scale Industrial Superconducting Quantum Computing

Source: The Quantum Insider April 03, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This significant funding milestone positions SpinQ as a major player in the global race to commercialize quantum computing, particularly noteworthy as China continues to heavily invest in quantum technologies as a strategic priority. The focus on "industrial superconducting quantum computing" suggests SpinQ is targeting practical enterprise applications rather than just research demonstrations, which could accelerate real-world quantum adoption. With nearly $139 million in total Series C funding, SpinQ joins the ranks of well-capitalized quantum companies like IonQ and Rigetti, indicating that the quantum computing market is maturing beyond pure venture capital into serious industrial investment. This funding level also reflects the growing confidence that quantum computing is transitioning from laboratory curiosity to commercially viable technology within the next decade.

— Mark Eatherly

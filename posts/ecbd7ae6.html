IonQ and Florida LambdaRail Launch U.S. Statewide Quantum-Safe Network

Source: Quantum Computing Report April 27, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership represents a significant milestone in the practical deployment of quantum-safe infrastructure, as Florida becomes the first U.S. state to implement quantum-secure communications at this scale. The collaboration between IonQ's quantum expertise and Florida LambdaRail's established fiber network demonstrates how quantum technologies are moving beyond research labs into real-world infrastructure protection. This initiative is particularly timely given growing concerns about quantum computers eventually breaking current encryption methods, making quantum-safe networks essential for protecting sensitive government and commercial communications. The statewide deployment could serve as a blueprint for other states looking to future-proof their digital infrastructure against the quantum threat.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

IonQ (NYSE: IONQ) and Florida LambdaRail (FLR) have announced a Master Service Agreement to deploy a quantum-safe communication network across the state of Florida. Announced at the 2026 eMerge Americas Conference, the initiative marks the first phase of a broader effort to transition critical fiber infrastructure toward physics-based, quantum-secure protection.

OVHcloud Adds Quandela Belenos to Quantum Platform

Source: The Quantum Insider April 17, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership marks a significant step in making photonic quantum computing more accessible through cloud infrastructure, as OVHcloud integrates Quandela's Belenos photonic quantum computer into their quantum platform. Photonic quantum systems offer unique advantages including room-temperature operation and natural compatibility with existing telecommunications infrastructure, making this cloud deployment particularly noteworthy for practical quantum applications. The collaboration between a major European cloud provider and a specialized photonic quantum company demonstrates the growing maturation of the quantum cloud ecosystem, where users can now access diverse quantum hardware architectures without massive capital investments. This move could accelerate research and development in photonic quantum computing by lowering barriers to entry for researchers and enterprises exploring quantum algorithms optimized for light-based systems.

— Mark Eatherly

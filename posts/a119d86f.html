Building advanced quantum chemistry calculations with Kvantify Qrunch on Amazon Braket

Source: AWS Quantum Computing Blog February 25, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights a crucial step toward making quantum chemistry simulations practical on today's NISQ devices, addressing one of quantum computing's most promising near-term applications. Kvantify Qrunch's integration with Amazon Braket represents the maturing ecosystem of quantum software tools, making sophisticated hybrid algorithms accessible to researchers who previously needed deep quantum programming expertise. The focus on chemistry calculations is particularly significant since molecular simulation could deliver some of the first real-world quantum advantages, potentially accelerating drug discovery and materials science research. By bridging the gap between theoretical quantum algorithms and practical implementation on cloud-accessible quantum hardware, this development brings us closer to seeing genuine quantum utility in computational chemistry.

— Mark Eatherly

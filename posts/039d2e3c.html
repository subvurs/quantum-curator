Building advanced quantum chemistry calculations with Kvantify Qrunch on Amazon Braket

Source: AWS Quantum Computing Blog February 25, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article showcases a practical milestone in making quantum chemistry accessible to researchers today, rather than waiting for fault-tolerant quantum computers. Kvantify Qrunch's integration with Amazon Braket represents the growing maturity of quantum cloud platforms, offering chemists and materials scientists user-friendly tools to explore molecular simulations on real NISQ hardware. The focus on hybrid algorithms is particularly significant because it bridges the gap between classical and quantum computing, allowing researchers to extract meaningful results from current noisy devices while building expertise for future breakthroughs. This democratization of quantum chemistry tools could accelerate drug discovery, catalyst design, and materials research across multiple industries.

— Mark Eatherly

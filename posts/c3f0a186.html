Xanadu Becomes First Pure-Play Photonic Quantum Computing Company to Go Public

Source: The Quantum Insider March 27, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This milestone marks a significant moment for the quantum computing industry, as Xanadu becomes the first company focused exclusively on photonic quantum computing to achieve public listing status. While IBM, Google, and other tech giants dominate headlines with their superconducting qubit systems, Xanadu's photonic approach offers unique advantages including room-temperature operation and natural compatibility with existing telecommunications infrastructure. The public listing provides Xanadu with increased capital to compete against well-funded rivals and validates photonic quantum computing as a serious contender in the race toward practical quantum advantage. This development signals growing investor confidence in alternative quantum computing architectures beyond the more established superconducting and trapped-ion platforms.

— Mark Eatherly

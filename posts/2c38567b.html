Quantum Design Acquires Quantum Sensing Company Qnami

Source: The Quantum Insider June 04, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This acquisition signals a significant consolidation in the quantum sensing market as established instrumentation giant Quantum Design absorbs diamond NV-center specialist Qnami, combining decades of materials characterization expertise with cutting-edge quantum sensing capabilities. The move reflects growing commercial interest in quantum sensing applications, particularly for semiconductor and materials research where diamond-based magnetometry can detect magnetic fields with unprecedented precision at the nanoscale. This merger could accelerate the deployment of quantum sensing tools in industrial settings, bridging the gap between academic research and practical applications in areas like quality control for quantum devices and advanced materials development. The acquisition also demonstrates how traditional scientific instrument companies are positioning themselves in the quantum technology ecosystem by incorporating quantum sensing into their broader portfolio of research tools.

— Mark Eatherly

Alice & Bob Expands Workforce to 251 Employees

Source: The Quantum Insider April 16, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This rapid workforce expansion at Alice & Bob signals serious momentum in the fault-tolerant quantum computing race, with the company completing ambitious hiring goals 30% ahead of schedule. The doubling of their team size in just seven months suggests strong investor confidence and aggressive scaling toward their cat qubit technology commercialization goals. This kind of talent acquisition velocity is particularly noteworthy in today's competitive quantum job market, where specialized quantum engineers and researchers are in extremely high demand. The expansion likely positions Alice & Bob to accelerate development of their unique approach to error correction, which could be crucial as the industry pushes toward practical quantum advantage in the coming years.

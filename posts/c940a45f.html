SpinQ Technology Secures Nearly 1 Billion Chinese Yuan in Series C Funding to Scale Industrial Superconducting Quantum Computing

Source: The Quantum Insider April 03, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This substantial funding round positions SpinQ Technology as a major player in the competitive quantum computing landscape, particularly significant given China's strategic push to establish quantum supremacy. The company's focus on superconducting quantum systems puts them in direct competition with industry leaders like IBM and Google, while their emphasis on full-stack solutions suggests they're building toward practical, deployable quantum computers rather than just research prototypes. With nearly $139 million in total Series C funding, SpinQ now has the capital to accelerate hardware development and potentially bridge the gap between laboratory demonstrations and industrial applications. This investment reflects the growing global recognition that quantum computing is transitioning from an academic curiosity to a critical technology with real commercial potential.

— Mark Eatherly

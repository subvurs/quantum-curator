NVision Secures $55 Million Series B to Advance Quantum Healthcare Platform

Source: The Quantum Insider May 13, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This significant Series B funding signals growing investor confidence in quantum sensing applications beyond traditional computing, particularly in healthcare where NVision's quantum sensors can detect biological processes at unprecedented sensitivity levels. Abbott's anchor investment is especially noteworthy as it suggests a major healthcare corporation sees commercial viability in quantum-enhanced diagnostic and therapeutic tools within a reasonable timeframe. The company's expansion into quantum computing represents an ambitious attempt to create a comprehensive quantum healthcare platform that could revolutionize drug discovery and personalized medicine by combining ultra-sensitive quantum sensors with quantum computational analysis. This funding milestone reflects the broader maturation of quantum technologies moving from research labs toward real-world medical applications.

— Mark Eatherly

Summary

Insider Brief PRESS RELEASE — NVision, a leader in quantum technologies for healthcare, today announced a $55 million Series B financing round anchored by Abbott. The company also announced a major expansion from quantum sensing into quantum computing, advancing its efforts to build an end to end, quantum based approach to designing and validating therapies.

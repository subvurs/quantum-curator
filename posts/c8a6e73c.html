This seed round signals that post‑quantum cryptography is moving from research labs into real‑world procurement pipelines, as investors and governments alike back startups that can deliver crypto‑agility at scale. pQCee’s funding arrives just as the NIST PQC standards are being finalized and major enterprises are drafting migration roadmaps, positioning the company to help bridge the gap between algorithm selection and seamless integration into existing security stacks.

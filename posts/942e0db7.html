Planckian Appoints Seth Lloyd as Founding Fellow

Source: The Quantum Insider May 14, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This appointment brings one of quantum computing's most influential theorists to Planckian, a startup focused on globally controlled quantum architectures that could offer advantages over traditional gate-based systems. Lloyd's expertise in quantum error correction theory will be crucial as Planckian tackles one of the field's biggest challenges: how to implement fault-tolerant quantum computing in architectures that don't follow the standard circuit model. The collaboration signals growing industry recognition that alternative quantum computing paradigms may need fundamentally different approaches to error correction, potentially opening new pathways to practical quantum advantage. Lloyd's involvement also lends significant credibility to Planckian's vision of using global control methods rather than individual qubit gates to perform quantum computations.

— Mark Eatherly

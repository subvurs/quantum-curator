QuantumCore Partners with Institute of Quantum Computing in $1.7 million Non-Dilutive Grant from NSERC

Source: The Quantum Insider April 27, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership between QuantumCore and the University of Waterloo's Institute for Quantum Computing represents a significant validation of industry-academia collaboration in quantum technology development, with the substantial $1.7 million NSERC grant providing crucial non-dilutive funding that won't require equity dilution for the startup. The Institute for Quantum Computing has been a powerhouse in quantum research for decades, and this alliance signals QuantumCore's serious commitment to translating academic breakthroughs into commercial quantum solutions. Non-dilutive grants like this are particularly valuable for quantum startups, which often face long development cycles and high capital requirements before reaching market viability. This type of government-supported partnership could serve as a model for accelerating quantum technology transfer from research labs to real-world applications across Canada's growing quantum ecosystem.

— Mark Eatherly

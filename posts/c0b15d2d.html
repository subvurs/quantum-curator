Dell Technologies Integrates Quantum-Ready Security and AI Resilience Across Portfolio

Source: Quantum Computing Report March 23, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This announcement signals a critical shift as major enterprise technology providers begin preparing for the post-quantum cryptographic transition that NIST recently standardized. Dell's portfolio-wide integration of quantum-resistant security measures reflects growing industry recognition that organizations need to start implementing PQC protections now, rather than waiting for large-scale quantum computers to emerge and potentially render current encryption vulnerable overnight. The convergence of AI and quantum threats that Dell addresses is particularly timely, as AI systems themselves could accelerate cryptanalytic attacks while quantum computers threaten the underlying mathematical assumptions of today's security infrastructure. This represents one of the most comprehensive enterprise-level responses to quantum cryptographic risks we've seen from a major technology vendor.

— Mark Eatherly

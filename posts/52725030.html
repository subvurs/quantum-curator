Cisco Unveils Prototype to Link Quantum Computers Into a Network

Source: The Quantum Insider April 24, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This prototype represents a crucial step toward building the quantum internet, where multiple quantum computers could work together as a distributed system rather than isolated machines. Cisco's universal quantum switch addresses one of quantum computing's biggest scaling challenges - current quantum computers are limited by the number of qubits they can reliably maintain, but networking them could effectively multiply computational power. The development is particularly significant because it comes from a networking giant with deep expertise in classical internet infrastructure, suggesting quantum networking is moving from academic research toward practical implementation. If successful, this technology could enable quantum cloud computing and allow smaller quantum devices to tackle problems that would otherwise require massive, monolithic quantum processors.

— Mark Eatherly

Fermilab Expands Quantum Workforce Pipeline with Student Program

Source: The Quantum Insider April 28, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This workforce development initiative at Fermilab addresses one of quantum computing's most pressing challenges: the severe shortage of qualified talent needed to advance the field. By engaging high school students through hands-on quantum education, the Saturday Morning Quantum program is creating a crucial early pipeline for future quantum scientists and engineers at a time when industry demand far outpaces supply. The program's timing is particularly strategic, as quantum technologies are transitioning from research labs to commercial applications, making early STEM education in quantum concepts essential for maintaining America's competitive edge in this critical technology sector.

— Mark Eatherly

Groove Quantum Demonstrates 18-Qubit Spin Processor and Secures Funding

Source: The Quantum Insider April 30, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This announcement marks a significant milestone for semiconductor spin qubits, which have long been considered the most promising pathway to scalable quantum computing due to their potential compatibility with existing silicon chip manufacturing. Groove Quantum's 18-qubit processor represents the largest spin-qubit system demonstrated to date, pushing beyond the typical 2-6 qubit prototypes that have dominated academic research and moving toward commercially relevant scales. The €16 million funding round signals growing investor confidence in spin-qubit technology as a viable alternative to the superconducting and trapped-ion approaches currently leading the field. Most importantly, this development suggests that spin qubits may finally be transitioning from laboratory curiosities to engineerable systems that could leverage decades of semiconductor industry expertise for mass production.

— Mark Eatherly

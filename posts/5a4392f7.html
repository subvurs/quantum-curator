Robert Loredo Named Executive Director of FAU Center for Quantum Technologies

Source: The Quantum Insider July 10, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article marks a strategic boost for the U.S. quantum ecosystem as FAU appoints Robert Loredo to steer its Center for Quantum Technologies at a time when academic hubs are racing to translate research into hardware and talent pipelines. Loredo’s industry experience—spanning IBM’s quantum services and workforce‑development programs—positions the center to deepen partnerships with chip makers, expand its new test‑bed facilities, and train the next generation of engineers needed for scaling error‑corrected processors. The appointment underscores Florida’s growing role in the national race to build a quantum supply chain, complementing recent federal investments and similar university initiatives across the country.

— Mark Eatherly

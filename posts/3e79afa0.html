OrangeQS Extends Seed Round to €15 Million And Partners With Rigetti, QuantWare and Peak Quantum

Source: The Quantum Insider April 21, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

OrangeQS's expanded funding round signals growing recognition that quantum chip testing infrastructure is becoming a critical bottleneck in the industry's scaling efforts. The company's partnerships with major players like Rigetti and QuantWare highlight how specialized testing capabilities are evolving from niche services to essential components of the quantum supply chain. This investment from the European Innovation Council also reflects Europe's strategic push to build domestic quantum infrastructure capabilities, potentially reducing reliance on foreign testing facilities as quantum processors become more sophisticated and commercially viable. The focus on testing infrastructure may seem less glamorous than breakthrough qubit achievements, but it represents the kind of foundational investment necessary for the quantum industry to mature beyond research laboratories.

— Mark Eatherly

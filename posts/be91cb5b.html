IQM and Real Asset Acquisition Move Forward with Public Listing Plans

Source: The Quantum Insider June 09, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This development marks a significant milestone for the quantum computing industry as IQM, one of Europe's leading quantum hardware companies, moves closer to becoming a publicly traded entity through its SPAC merger with Real Asset Acquisition Corp. The public listing would provide IQM with enhanced capital access to scale their superconducting quantum computer technology and compete more effectively with established players like IBM and Google in the global quantum market. As one of the few pure-play quantum computing companies pursuing public markets, IQM's listing could serve as a bellwether for investor appetite in quantum technologies and potentially pave the way for other quantum startups to follow suit. This move comes at a critical time when quantum hardware companies need substantial funding to bridge the gap between current noisy intermediate-scale quantum devices and the fault-tolerant systems needed for commercial quantum advantage.

— Mark Eatherly

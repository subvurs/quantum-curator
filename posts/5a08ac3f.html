Overview of the Italian Quantum Ecosystem

Source: Quantum Computing Report June 06, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This comprehensive overview of Italy's quantum ecosystem offers valuable insights into one of Europe's most rapidly developing quantum programs, which has been gaining momentum through significant government investments and academic partnerships. Italy's strategic positioning in quantum technologies, including its participation in major EU quantum initiatives and growing startup ecosystem, makes this analysis particularly timely as the country emerges as a key player alongside quantum leaders like Germany and the Netherlands. The report from Politecnico di Milano's Quantum Computing & Communication Observatory provides crucial context for understanding how national quantum strategies are evolving across Europe and competing globally with initiatives from the US and China. For investors and policymakers, this deep dive into Italy's quantum landscape reveals both opportunities and challenges in a market that's becoming increasingly important for technological sovereignty and economic competitiveness.

— Mark Eatherly

Building advanced quantum chemistry calculations with Kvantify Qrunch on Amazon Braket

Source: AWS Quantum Computing Blog February 25, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article showcases a significant step toward making quantum chemistry practically accessible on today's NISQ devices through Kvantify's specialized software stack on Amazon Braket. By focusing on hybrid algorithms that can work effectively despite current hardware limitations, this represents the kind of pragmatic approach the field needs to bridge the gap between quantum computing's theoretical promise and real-world chemical simulation applications. The integration with AWS's cloud quantum platform could democratize access to advanced quantum chemistry calculations for researchers and companies who previously lacked the expertise to implement these complex algorithms from scratch. This development is particularly noteworthy as chemistry simulations remain one of the most compelling near-term applications for quantum advantage, with potential impacts spanning drug discovery, materials science, and catalyst design.

— Mark Eatherly

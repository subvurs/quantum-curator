Purdue University Launches Comprehensive Quantum Degrees Program

Source: Quantum Computing Report May 14, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This article highlights a crucial development in addressing the quantum industry's talent bottleneck, as Purdue becomes one of the first major universities to launch a comprehensive multi-tiered quantum degree program spanning both engineering and science disciplines. The timing is particularly significant given the rapid expansion of quantum companies and research initiatives across the Midwest's "Quantum Prairie" region, where demand for qualified quantum professionals far exceeds supply. Purdue's structured approach to quantum education could serve as a model for other institutions grappling with how to prepare students for an industry that requires deep technical expertise spanning physics, computer science, and engineering. This represents a critical step toward building the sustainable workforce needed to support the quantum industry's transition from research laboratories to commercial applications.

— Mark Eatherly

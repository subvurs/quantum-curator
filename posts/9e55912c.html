King’s College London Awarded Access to Google’s Willow Processor Through NQCC Initiative

Source: The Quantum Insider May 28, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This partnership represents a significant step in democratizing access to Google's cutting-edge Willow quantum processor, which recently demonstrated breakthrough error correction capabilities that could pave the way toward fault-tolerant quantum computing. By providing King's College London with direct access through the NQCC initiative, Google is fostering academic research that could uncover novel quantum applications beyond the typical focus areas of cryptography and optimization. The collaboration signals a maturing quantum ecosystem where leading tech companies are strategically partnering with universities to accelerate practical quantum algorithm development, potentially bridging the gap between today's noisy intermediate-scale quantum devices and tomorrow's fault-tolerant quantum computers.

— Mark Eatherly

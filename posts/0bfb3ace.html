NVIDIA Launches Ising, the World’s First Open AI Models to Accelerate The Path to Useful Quantum Computers

Source: The Quantum Insider April 14, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

NVIDIA's launch of Ising represents a fascinating convergence of AI and quantum computing that could accelerate progress toward fault-tolerant quantum systems. By open-sourcing AI models specifically designed to tackle quantum processor calibration and error correction - two of the biggest bottlenecks preventing useful quantum applications - NVIDIA is democratizing access to sophisticated tools that were previously available only to well-funded research teams. This move reflects the growing recognition that classical AI will play a crucial role in making quantum computers practical, particularly in managing the complex control systems needed to maintain quantum coherence at scale. The open-source approach could spark innovation across the quantum ecosystem by enabling smaller research groups and startups to leverage NVIDIA's expertise in optimizing quantum hardware performance.

— Mark Eatherly

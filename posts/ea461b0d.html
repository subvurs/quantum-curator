ICQIA Expands Global Reach With New Charter And Members

Source: The Quantum Insider June 10, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This expansion of the International Council of Quantum Industry Associations represents a crucial step in creating coordinated global governance for the rapidly maturing quantum industry. With UKQuantum and the Korea Quantum Industry Association joining the council, ICQIA now spans key quantum powerhouses across multiple continents, creating a more comprehensive framework for addressing international standards, trade policies, and collaborative research initiatives. This kind of cross-border coordination becomes increasingly vital as quantum technologies approach commercial viability and nations seek to balance competitive advantages with the need for interoperable systems and shared safety standards. The timing is particularly significant as governments worldwide are ramping up quantum investments and need unified industry voices to guide policy decisions that will shape the global quantum ecosystem.

— Mark Eatherly

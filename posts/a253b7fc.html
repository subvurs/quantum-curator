OQC, JPMorganChase and AMD Commence Research Collaboration to Develop New Quantum-AI Platform in London

Source: The Quantum Insider June 05, 2026 Curated by Mark Eatherly

Curator's Take

This collaboration represents a significant step toward practical quantum-AI integration, with JPMorganChase becoming one of the first major financial institutions to test quantum algorithms in a dedicated enterprise-grade environment rather than relying solely on cloud access. The partnership combines OQC's quantum hardware expertise with AMD's classical computing infrastructure, creating a hybrid platform that could accelerate the development of quantum advantage applications in financial modeling and risk analysis. What makes this particularly noteworthy is the focus on building secure, on-premises quantum infrastructure in London, potentially establishing a template for how other enterprises might deploy quantum computing capabilities within their own data centers rather than depending entirely on external quantum cloud services.

— Mark Eatherly

Insider Brief PRESS RELEASE — European leader in the cloud and co-founding member of France Quantum, is participating in the fifth edition of the event. With more than 1,500 visitors and 60 speakers, France Quantum 2026 brings together European specialists in quantum computing for a day of conferenc...

This partnership brings together Welinq's quantum interconnect technology with OVHCloud's European cloud infrastructure to develop networked quantum computing architectures that could distribute quantum processing across multiple nodes rather than relying on single monolithic processors. The collaboration addresses one of quantum computing's key scaling challenges by exploring how to link quantum systems together, potentially enabling larger effective quantum computers than current hardware limits allow. As a major European cloud provider backing quantum networking, OVHCloud's involvement signals growing commercial confidence that distributed quantum architectures represent a viable path to practical quantum advantage. The announcement at France Quantum 2026 underscores Europe's coordinated push to build a complete quantum technology stack from hardware interconnects through cloud delivery.

Joined-up thinking in quantum metrology: why collaboration is the secret of success

NPL has joined forces with other leading National Metrology Institutes to shape the international standards effort in quantum technologies The post Joined-up thinking in quantum metrology: why collaboration is the secret of success appeared first on Physics World .

This collaboration between National Metrology Institutes marks a crucial step in establishing the measurement standards that will underpin the quantum technology industry's growth. As quantum sensors and other quantum devices move from research labs toward commercial applications, having internationally agreed-upon standards for calibrating and validating their performance becomes essential for trade, regulation, and consumer confidence. The involvement of NPL and other leading metrology organizations signals that the quantum industry is maturing beyond proof-of-concept demonstrations toward the standardization phase that typically precedes widespread adoption. This standardization effort could accelerate quantum technology deployment by ensuring devices from different manufacturers can be meaningfully compared and that quantum-enhanced measurements are trusted across borders and industries.
